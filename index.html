Music & Arts 12 Hour Marathon
Support your neighbors affected by ICE!

When: Saturday, March 7th, 10AM-10PM

Where: The Grand Event Center

Although the event will last for a whole twelve hours, come whenever you can! There will be live music from Northfield bands like Wintry Elementary and Her.man, multiple silent auctions of local art, and more. Proceeds will go to Sharing Our Roots and local mutual aid efforts.
